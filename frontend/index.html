Native Architecture vs. Old Paradigms

The 100ms Speech-to-Speech Revolution

Traditional AI voice receptionists feel frustratingly slow because they stitch separate models together. Grok Voice Think Fast 1.0 streams audio natively for immediate, natural dialogue.

TRADITIONAL Pipeline Chained STT ➔ LLM ➔ TTS

The Fractured Journey

Incoming audio packets are fully transcribed to text. That text is sent to an LLM, processed, and response text is compiled. Finally, a text-to-speech voice generator parses and reads the audio. This creates an awkward 5-second silence.

1. Speech-to-Text (STT Transcription) 1,800ms
2. Large Language Model Reasoning 2,100ms
3. Text-to-Speech Generation 1,500ms
TOTAL LAG: ~5.4 Seconds
