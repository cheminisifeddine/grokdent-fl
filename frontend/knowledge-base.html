General Active

What services does your dental clinic offer?

We offer a full range of dental services including general dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, orthodontics, pediatric dentistry, emergency dental care, periodontics, endodontics, oral surgery, and teeth whitening. We are committed to providing comprehensive dental care for the whole family.

Hours Active

What are your office hours?

We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. For dental emergencies outside of business hours, please call our emergency line at (305) 555-0199.

Insurance Active

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major dental insurance plans including Delta Dental, Humana, MCNA Dental, Guardian, Cigna, MetLife, Aetna, and Florida Blue. We also offer flexible payment plans for patients without insurance. Please call us for specific coverage questions.

Emergency Active

What should I do if I have a dental emergency?

If you're experiencing a dental emergency such as severe tooth pain, a knocked-out tooth, or significant swelling, please call us immediately at (305) 555-0100. During office hours, we accommodate same-day emergency appointments. After hours, call our emergency line at (305) 555-0199. If you have life-threatening symptoms, please call 911.
